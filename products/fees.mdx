Axis has three fee streams.

---

## Fee Summary

| Fee | Rate | Who pays | Who receives |
|---|---|---|---|
| Swap fee | 0.25% | Investor | Protocol treasury |
| AUM fee | 0.20% / year | ETF holders | Protocol treasury |
| MEV auction yield | Winning bid | Jito searcher | Protocol treasury |

<Note>
Creator fees are not enabled in the current Mainnet MVP. All fee revenue goes to the protocol treasury.
</Note>

---

## Swap Fee (0.25%)

Charged on every swap into or out of an ETF. Applies to both invest and redeem transactions.

The entire 0.25% goes to the protocol treasury in the current MVP.

---

## AUM Fee (0.20% annually)

A continuous fee accrued on the total value of assets held in ETF pools. Collected by the protocol.

This is charged on Mainnet. On Devnet, the AUM fee is present but has no real value.

---

## MEV Auction Yield (PFDA)

The PFDA batch auction forces MEV searchers to compete for the right to clear each batch. The winning searcher submits a bid via Jito bundle. This bid flows directly to the protocol treasury.

This converts MEV extraction — which in standard AMMs bleeds value from LPs — into yield that stays in the protocol.

On Devnet, the Jito searcher network is not active and this yield is not generated.
