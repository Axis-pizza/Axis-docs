## Non-Custodial Design

Axis, Inc. has no custody over user funds at any point. Assets are held in on-chain smart contract vaults. The team cannot freeze, seize, or recover user funds.

Withdrawals are always available by calling `withdraw` directly against the on-chain program, regardless of frontend availability.
