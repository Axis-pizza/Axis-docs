## Closed Beta Terms

<Note>
Axis Mainnet is in closed, invite-only beta. Formal Terms of Service will be published before Mainnet V1 public launch. The following summarizes key terms applicable during the closed beta.
</Note>

**Eligibility.** Closed beta access is granted by invite only. Invite codes are non-transferable. The team reserves the right to revoke access at any time.

## Privacy

Axis is a non-custodial, pseudonymous protocol. The primary identifier is your Solana wallet address. The team does not collect personal information beyond what is technically necessary to operate the service (e.g., wallet addresses associated with invite codes).

On-chain transactions are public and permanent on the Solana blockchain.
