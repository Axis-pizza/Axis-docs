Axis is a permissionless on-chain ETF platform built on Solana. Anyone can tokenize a basket of crypto assets as a tradeable ETF in seconds — and anyone can invest in one with a single swipe.

  **Entity in formation.** Axis is currently operated by the founding team. The legal entities referenced below (Axis, Inc. and Axis Foundation) are planned but not yet incorporated. References to "Axis" in this document refer to the team operating the protocol during closed beta.
</Warning>

## Core Technology

Axis is powered by the **PFDA (Protocol Fee Discount Auction)** mechanism — a periodic batch auction that batches user swap requests, clears them at a single price every 10 slots (~4 seconds on Solana), and forces MEV searchers to compete for the right to clear the batch. The winning bid flows back to the protocol as yield, not to extractors.
